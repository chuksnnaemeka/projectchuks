Dandy Heart Foundation

Working with basic research work, the foundation tends to improve the life & health state of people living across Africa

We promote the care, well-being and development of all children, widows and less privileged people, in need especially the deprived, the abused, the destitute and the homeless. To create awareness among the general public of the existence of children and widows in need. To ensure that no Nigerian child, widow is left homeless, unprotected, in danger or exploited. To provide counselling, educational and vocational skills training for children and persons in need to enable them to become self-supporting in various trades and occupations. To work towards provision of basic health care for children and other less privilege living or subsisting on the streets, especially those suffering from physical and mental handicaps and debilitating diseases.
