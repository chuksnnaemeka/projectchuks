PURPOSE The organisation is established for charitable purposes only. The organisation and its endowments shall be administered in conformity with the provision of the companies an Allied Matter Act, 1990. The Trustees shall hold the trust fund and income thereof upon trust to apply the same for the purpose and in the manner hereinafter appearing but only if and in so far as such purpose shall be charitable. The Organisation shall be non-profit and non-political. OBJECTIVES In furtherance of the purpose hereinbefore defined, the objectives of the organisation are as follows: To promote the care, well-being and development of all children, widows and less privileged people, in need especially the deprived, the abused, the destitute and the homeless. To create awareness among the general public of the existence of children and widows in need. To ensure that no Nigerian child, widow is left homeless, unprotected, in danger or exploited. To provide counselling, educational and vocational skills training for children and persons in need to enable them to become self-supporting in various trades and occupations. To work towards provision of basic health care for children and other less privilege living or subsisting on the streets, especially those suffering from physical and mental handicaps and debilitating diseases. To work with other concerned citizens and organisations to protect children from abuse and oppression and ensure that enforcement of children’s legal rights. To help in providing facilities and materials in schools, centers, children homes, orphanages and other similar establishment.
